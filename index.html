Russian Sleep Experiment

Russian Sleep Experiment: The Russian Sleep Experiment is a creepypasta about a secret Soviet experiment where prisoners were kept awake for 15 days. As sleep deprivation took its toll, the subjects became violent and deranged.

Plague Doctor

Plague Doctor (SCP-049): SCP-049, known as the Plague Doctor, is a humanoid entity in the SCP Foundation that resembles a 17th-century plague doctor. It believes it is curing a disease called the "Pestilence," but its methods involve killing and reanimating victims.

Sirenhead

Sirenhead: Sirenhead is a towering, monstrous creature with sirens for a head, capable of emitting distorted sounds and mimicking voices. Created by artist Trevor Henderson, Sirenhead is a modern horror icon known for its eerie and unsettling presence.
